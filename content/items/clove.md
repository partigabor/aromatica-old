The dried flower-buds of a tropical tree from the Moluccas, Indonesia; bud of Syzygium aromaticum. Clove (*Syzygium aromaticum* (L.) Merr. \& L.M.Perry) is a culinary and medicinal spice from the *Myrtaceae* family,[^powo] originating in the region(s) of N. Moluccas, Indonesia. It is used for its flower buds, primarily for meats, sauces; anesthetic. Its aroma is described as sweet, pungent, astringent, with a heat index of 5.[^ucla_medicinal_2002]
