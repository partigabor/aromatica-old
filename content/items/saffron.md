SAFFRON -- Crocus sativus L. -- is a(n) tuberous geophyte in the *Iridaceae* family, growing in the subtropical biome, with a native range of a cultigen from Greece. It is used as a(n) culinary spice; dye, cultivated for its stigma (style).
