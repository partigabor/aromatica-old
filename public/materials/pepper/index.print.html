Pepper

Illustration of Piper nigrum L. from Köhler’s Medizinal-Pflanzen (1887), II 144.

Pepper (Piper nigrum L.) is a culinary and medicinal spice from the Piperaceae family,1 originating in the region(s) of Malabar coast (South India).2 It is used for its fruit, primarily for flavors; colds; pepper steak; poivrade sauce; Pfefferkuchen. Its aroma is described as pungent, hot, with a heat index of 3-8.3
